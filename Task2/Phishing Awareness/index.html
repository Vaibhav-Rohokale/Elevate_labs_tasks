Suspicious Email Example

From: support@bank-secure-login.com

Subject: Urgent: Verify Your Account

Dear User,
Your account will be suspended in 24 hours due to unusual activity. Click here to verify now.

❌ This is phishing! Suspicious domain, urgency, and fake link.

Safety Tips

  • ✔ Check the sender’s email carefully.
  • ✔ Hover over links before clicking.
  • ✔ Look for HTTPS and known domains.
  • ✔ Don’t share personal info via suspicious links.
  • ✔ Report suspicious messages.
